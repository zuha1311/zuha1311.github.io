Blood Bank

A real-time blood donor locating app which notifies the nearest donors for blood requirement.


Client: Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, AMU, India

Role: UX Researcher, UX Designer, Developer

Thought Process

❝
What are the problems associated with static blood bank applications?

Why are the current systems of blood donation not being utilized to their full potential?

How can these problems be solved to ensure the timely donation of blood?

❞
Vision
The current systems allow only the static details of donors to be maintained by blood banks and hospitals. Such systems, provide a list of donors based on their contact location which they provided during registration. In real scenarios, the actual location of the donor could be different than the registered address and the distance from the patient’s site.

The proposed system will help the requester (patient) or hospitals locate nearby donors (volunteers) and forward the request for help. The requester must specify the blood group and the system will find the nearest volunteers having a compatible blood group. The periodic geo-location updates of the volunteer’s mobile device will help the system find the nearest donors quickly. Also, it will ensure the privacy of user data.

Information Architecture
The main aim of the information architecture is to:

1. Reduce the time to make a request from 6 minutes to < 2 minutes.
2. Ensure transparent communication between requester and donor.
3. Ensure only verified volunteers can donate blood.
