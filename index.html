BloodLink
7% increase in monthly blood donations in 3 months

Static donor information based on registered contact details often leads to inaccurate location information, causing delays in blood donation. To address this issue, a real-time blood donor-locating app can be designed to provide a user-friendly interface that allows hospitals and patients to locate nearby donors efficiently. The app should enable users to specify blood groups and receive a list of nearby volunteers with compatible blood types. To improve the user experience, the app could incorporate periodic geo-location updates from the volunteer's mobile device, ensuring quick and efficient blood donation. Additionally, the app should maintain user privacy, ensuring that sensitive data is secure.
